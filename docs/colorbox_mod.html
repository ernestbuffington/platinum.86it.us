Platinum Nuke Pro Ckeditor Colorbox mod.

With JQuery Colorbox and our colorbox mod for the Ckeditor you have a fast, easy and painless way to display large, and album style images on your website. With a few clicks and keystrokes you will up and running.

See below for a basic how to of colorbox features.

  • In the Ckeditor you can upload, add, edit or choose your image. Click on the image-add/edit in the editor (See Example)
    upload, add or choose your image. (See Example) Adjust image size set alignment and add Alternative Text (See Example) .

  • Now click on the link tab (See Example) . On this tab you will need to either browse and pick the image you did on the previous tab or you can copy and past the image path (URL) information, you will need to give the image a colorbox title and choose a colorbox setting (See Example) . After you have completed filling in the information required click ok and you will return to the editor page(See Example) save, that's all there is to it.

  • If you did things right it should look something like this when the image is clicked on. (See Example).

    Note: you can re-size your image as small as you like the colorbox will show original image size.
