Common Nuke Sentinel Issues

Nuke Sentinel can be one of the best security features you can use on your site but it can also be one of the hardest to get setup so you need to follow the install and setup directions carefully.

The Most common issue is getting yourself banned from your own site. I know, it sounds impossible to do right but it has happened to the best of us. If you do ban yourself you will need to do one of 2 things.

  • You can login to your cPanel and go to your PHPmyAdmin and edit your IP Ban table by removing your IP Address.
  • You can open your root/includes/nukesentinel.php and look for these lines (See Example) and comment them out like (See Example) and save and reupload to your site.
  • You can also check in your .htaccess file and see if your IP Address has been stored there and remove it. If you are not sure what your IP is just visit What is my IP.

If none of the above steps get you back on your site please visit the Platinum Nuke Website for support.
