Add to Cart buttons enable your buyers to add items to your PayPal Shopping Cart while browsing your entire site. When finished, your buyers can make their purchases quickly and securely on PayPal-hosted payment pages.
To generate the HTML for your Add to Cart button:
  • Choose a button
  • Enter your email and product information
  • Click "Create HTML"
  • Select and copy the HTML code
  • Place the HTML code into your web page
  • Your customers will see the 'Add to Cart' button
