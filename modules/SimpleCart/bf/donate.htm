Donate buttons enable your donors to donate from your web site. When finished, donors can make their donations quickly and securely on PayPal-hosted payment pages.
To generate the HTML for your Donate button:
  • Choose a button
  • Enter your email and product information
  • Click "Create HTML"
  • Select and copy the HTML code
  • Place the HTML code into your web page
  • Your customers will see the 'Donate' button
